What is Manage Cabbage?

Manage Cabbage is a personal finance management tool. It allows you to log and analyse your spending with helpful tools to categorise and organise your data.

Think of it as equivelant to a spreadsheet. Although unlike a spreadsheet, data is easier to input and you don't need to decide how the data will be organised.

Currently the app is only available on Android devices and is still in early development.
